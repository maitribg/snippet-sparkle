â“ Frequently Asked Questions

Where are my snippets stored?

In your browser's localStorage. Each browser/device has its own separate storage.

Will my snippets sync across devices?

No, not automatically. Use Export/Import to transfer snippets between devices.

What if I clear my browser data?

Your snippets will be lost! Always export as a backup before clearing browser data.

Can multiple people share snippets?

Yes! Export your snippets and share the JSON file with your team. They can import it into their browser.

Is there a limit to how many snippets I can create?

localStorage limits vary by browser (usually 5-10MB). You can easily store hundreds of snippets.
